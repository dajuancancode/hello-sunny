Discover Fort Lauderdale

Greater Fort Lauderdale is paradise, home to 23 miles of beaches, the area is sunny year round. We invite you to come and visit with us. You can enjoy one of our many picturesque nature perserves, or you can endulge your shopaholic addiction in the largest flea market in the world, the swap shop. Fort Lauderdale has everything you could ever whish for, so join us.

Attractions

Enjoy the Nightlife

The city is known for its nightlife. Whether you want to dance the night away, or just want to take in some nice music, we have just the place for you.

Take in a Game

Greater Fort Lauderdale is home of the National Hockey League's Florida Panthers. While you're here, you might want to check out a game.

Visit a Museum

We love the arts and sciences. While you're here, you should make time to visit one of our Museums, like the NSU Museum of Art.

Commune with Nature

The Greater Fort Lauderdale area has quite a few different nature preserves. There's Butterfly World in Coconut Creek, ore Flamingo Gardens in Davie.

Relax on the Beach

There is no way you could come to Fort Lauderdale and not visit a beach. Each of our beaches has their on unique charactersitics, so we're sure you'll find one you like.

Take a Cruise

Fort Lauderdale's seaport, Port Everglades is worlds 3rd busiest cruise port. You can even take one day cruises to the Bahamas.
